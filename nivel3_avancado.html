Q3Complete o diálogo+1pt
— Teacher: "Do you like cheese, Ana?"
— Ana não gosta. Complete com don't ou doesn't:

"No, I _______ like cheese."
💡 Dica: Para negar com I/you/we/they → don't · Para he/she/it → doesn't
